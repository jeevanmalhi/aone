Job Opportunities

We help with jobs upon graduation

The hairstyling and aesthetics industry is exponentially growing. Excellent career opportunities await those who have the aspirations of becoming successful professionals. However, the success of your career strongly depends on the training and instruction methods provided in preparing you for an exciting and challenging future. The hairstyling industry has come a long way as it is growing and seeking highly skilled professionals who practice a more holistic approach towards health, beauty and wellness. From being a salon owner to expressing your creativity as a hair stylist for runway models – sky’s the limit.

At A-One we offer the best, skilled industry professionals who are enthusiastic about trends and our exciting industry to teach and inspire our wonderful students. A Diploma from A-One will open the doors to a bright and successful career in numerous professions such as Salon Aesthetician, Home Business offering Aesthetic Services, Hair Product Consultant, Hair Stylist and many more.

We also pride ourselves on our energetic and creative teams of positive employees, who share our vision and passion for hair and esthetics. A-One offers exciting career opportunities at our Brampton location. Check back for future postings!
